Overveiw

The residential decarbonization groups all home efficiency, electric appliances, and clean energy upgrades under one single goal: eliminating a household's fossil fuel footprint.


Why

By decarbonizing your house the total lifecycle greenhouse gas emissions drops from 1,013 metric tons down to 374 metric tons, making the house more environmnet friendly.

Financial

The average household in Indianapolis served by AES Indiana pays approximately $158 to $226 per month just for electricity, alongside average natural gas bills of roughly $122 per month depending on seasonal spikes. - IN.gov
